DHA T VIKA

The Annual Metallurgical Fest of BIT Sindri

A BOUT
Dhatvika, the annual metallurgy festival of BIT Sindri, has been a remarkable gathering point for academic learning, pioneering research, and professional industry practices since it first began in 2015. This event, marked by a significant audience, experienced speakers, engaging dialogues, and highly competitive activities, has created a unique environment for the integration of aspiring and established metallurgists. The difficulties we confront in Metallurgy are indeed substantial and intricate. Dhatvika's purpose is to establish a collaborative intersection between various segments of the metallurgy fraternity in order to address these pressing issues earnestly and swiftly. Dhatvika stands as an engaging festival that emphasizes the vibrant dimensions of metallurgical engineering and material science, which is a vital competency of our institution.
